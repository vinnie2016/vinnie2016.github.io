What We Do

The ADEA Application Services is a centralized application service for advanced dental education programs. Save time and energy by completing one application for multiple programs. While advanced dental education programs accept the ADEA PASS, AADSAS, DHCAS, and CAAPID applications, they all have different requirements, deadlines and admissions timelines.
